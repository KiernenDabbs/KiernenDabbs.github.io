About Me

Personal History

Professional History

In the summer of 2023, I started my career in technology when I was hired at the Waterloo Community Unit School District in Waterloo, Illinois. I was proud to work at the school of my hometown and it was a great area to get my feet wet in the IT field.

In Fall of 2023, I started my education at Ranken Technical College. I started working with front end development likeHTML and CSS, then I learned frameworks like Bootstrap and the flexbox system. I've been coding websites ever since!
